Caleb's Current Projects

UGA's Cubesat - Spacey Sciences


I am the student leader for the University of Georgia's CubeSat project. I am leading a team of undergraduates to launch a small nano-satellite into Low Earth Orbit. This will be the first satellite launched by my University and the first NASA CubeSat from the state of Georgia. More information about DAWGSat-1 can be found about Spacey.io's information page.

Core Flight Software - NASA Development


I help design audio systems for NASA's Orion Spacecraft. The Core Flight Software and and other Open Source NASA projects can be found on NASA's github page here github.com/nasa.

Remote Telescopes - Spacey Sciences


I am the founder of Spacey.io, a company that is dedicated to making astronomy accesible to all. We have built out first prototype telescope which can be remote controled with a website. We built this product at VTHacks and ended up winning the Hackathon.

High Altitude Balloons - Satellite Testing


My company - Spacey.io - is designing my university's first satellite. In order to test that our satellite part are working correctly we send them 90km up in high altitude balloons.

Google Glass Research


I have worked for a fortune 30 company to research the Retail applications of Google Glass. I currently work as an Undergraduate Researcher at UGA's Hodgson Glass Research Lab. I assist Dr. Turner in finding applications for emerging technologies in a classical music setting.

Smart Podium Research - Raspberry Pi


I am currently writing booting and configuration scripts for a raspberry pi that will serve as a smart music podium. I am hoping to interface this raspberry pi with pairs of google glass to communicate music between many people.

RepRap 3D Open Source Printer


I am currently building my own 3D printer. The printer is an open source model from the RepRap Project. The building of this printer requires knowlege of arduino programming and computer engineering. I will be leading a 3D printer hackathon build with UGA's IEEE engineering club later this year.

Automation Scripts for Minecraft


I am currently writing Bash and Ruby scripts that can sit on top of a Linux Minecraft server and monitor data. The goal of this project is to have a functional Bash shell within the chat log of Minecraft. My progress on this project can be monitored at my AutoScript repository on github.

Simple Virtual Reality


I have first hand experience with Google Cardboard and the creation of Virtual Reality spaces. I have programed my own worlds in OpenGL ES 2.0, constructed shaders, and built simple engines that optimize object drawing.
